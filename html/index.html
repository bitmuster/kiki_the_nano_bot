Homepage

Welcome to the homepage of 'kiki the nano bot'.

kiki the nano bot is a 3-D puzzle game, basically a mixture of the games Sokoban and Kula-World.

kiki has won the Categories 'Best Graphics', 'Best Originality' and 'Best Overall Game' in the uDevGame Game Programming Contest 2002.
